Since MJ was one of the greatest players you can only imagine all the shoe offers he had. He choose to sign with nike. In 1984 the first model of the Air Jordan was released.

The new model of the Jordan one came out in 1985. From this start Jordan and Nike produced new number shoes because of the success in the first shoe. This shoe was released in 1986. Following that came the Jordan III. This shoe was special because this shoe had a jumpman logo on it of Micheal Jordan. This shoes was released in 1988. More and more number Jordans were produced. A total of 23 different retro shoes were produced.
